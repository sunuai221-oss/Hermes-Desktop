# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

Please keep participation in Hermes Desktop respectful, constructive, and professional.

Expected behavior:

- be respectful in discussion and review
- focus on the technical issue, not the person
- accept feedback in good faith
- help keep the project welcoming to new contributors

Unacceptable behavior:

- harassment or personal attacks
- abusive or discriminatory language
- deliberate disruption of discussion or review
- publishing private information without permission

## Scope

This applies to issues, pull requests, discussions, and other project spaces.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions and interactions that do not meet this standard.

Serious concerns can be reported privately through GitHub.
